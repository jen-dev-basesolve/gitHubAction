what are github actions?
- They are series of actions when github is triggered. They are used to automate workflows.

workflows - 
- Automated process that runs multiple jobs
- they are stored in ./github/workflows

what are webhooks?
webhooks are automated messages sent from apps whenevher there is a trigger.
